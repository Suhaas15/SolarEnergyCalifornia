Project Size Categories

Solar installations are categorized by land area to help analyze efficiency patterns and environmental impact across different scales of deployment.

Small Projects

< 1 acre

Typically residential rooftop installations and small commercial systems. These projects maximize urban space utilization and provide distributed energy generation.

Medium Projects

1 - 5 acres

Commercial and small utility-scale ground-mount installations. Ideal for business complexes, schools, and community solar projects.

Large Projects

5 - 10 acres

Medium utility-scale solar farms and large commercial complexes. Balance between significant energy production and manageable land use.

X-Large Projects

10+ acres

Large utility-scale solar installations and solar parks. Major renewable energy contributors with significant land footprint considerations.

Analysis Insight

Different project sizes have unique efficiency characteristics, environmental impacts, and economic considerations. Use the size filter to explore how scale affects sustainability metrics and land use patterns.
